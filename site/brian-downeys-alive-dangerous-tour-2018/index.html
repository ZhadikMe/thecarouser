Brian Downey’s Alive & Dangerous Announce Tour

By Mandy Morello @mandy_morello · On April 13, 2018

Brian Downey’s new Thin Lizzy band is hitting the road again for the 40th anniversary of Live And Dangerous.

Due to the success of their previous dates, the band have announced a Live and Dangerous tour in the UK this September and October. They’ll be performing Thin Lizzy’s Live & Dangerous in its entirety. This is one of the most popular live albums of the ’70s.

Brian Downey says, “We will be celebrating the 40th Anniversary of Live and Dangerous by performing the album in its entirety when we tour it in the UK in September and October. Thin Lizzy fans have been asking us when we will embark on a UK tour. The time has finally arrived.”

In an exclusive interview with The Carouser, Brian revealed that Alive And Dangerous have been working on some new material too.

Tickets for their new tour will go on sale from today and can be purchased directly from the venues.

Alive & Dangerous Uk Tour 2018

Tuesday 25 September – South Shields, Queen Vic

Thursday 27 September – Stoke, Eleven

Friday 28 September – London, Camden Underworld

Saturday 29 September – Wolverhampton, The Robin 2

Monday 1 October – Glasgow, Classic Grand
