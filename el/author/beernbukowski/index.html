Το Ιρλανδικό Ζήτημα

Our 9th magazine celebrates all things Irish. From Irish rock 'n' roll to the hard stuff- whiskey. We speak to Phil Lynott's mother and Sean O'Connor from The Lookalikes about the '70s. We navigate through Celtic Punk and, of course, the beer. Discover Ιρλανδία through the pages of our limited edition magazine.
